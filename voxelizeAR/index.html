Turn the world into Minecraft-style cubes using augmented reality

About Documentation

Voxelize Reality

Touch the screen to convert the world around you into little 3D cubes, creating a unique lo-fi effect.

Because this is an augmented reality app, the effect persists even when you walk around the world.

Placement

Scale the voxel grid up for a more blocky appearance or scale it down for more detail. Touch and drag to rotate the voxel grid so it best aligns with the real world.

Devices with a LiDAR sensor — such as the iPhone 12 Pro or iPad Pro — can voxelize any, three dimensional surfaces!
