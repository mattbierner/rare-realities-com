Turn any surface into water using augmented reality

About Documentation

Waves

Transform streets, fields, and walls into roiling oceans with 3D wave distortions.

Adjust the wave effects for the perfect nautical look!

Rain

Distort surfaces with raindrops. Each drop lifts the surface as it hits and spreads outwards.

The rain drops are physically simulated making every simulation unique.

Touch

Watch ripples spread out as you run your hand along a wall or floor with the interactive surface feature. This lets you physically interact with surfaces as if they were made of water.

Note that interactive surface requires a device with a LiDAR sensor
